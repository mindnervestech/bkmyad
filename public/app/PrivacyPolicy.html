    Does website send cookies?
"Cookies" are small files placed on your hard drive that assist us in providing our services. Yes, we send cookies to your computer. these cookies are sent to know your search behaviour on our website and to make improvement according to you. you can disable using your browser settings. But in that case, you will not be able to access our all services and products.

All personal and sensitive information of users such as bank details are transferred under secured socket layer in encrypted form and then integrated into the payment gateway. Once again, we mention that as soon as the order has been booked, your bank details are removed from our servers.
