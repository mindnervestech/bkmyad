About Adschela.com :
The only way to stand firmly in the market is to keep continually updating with technology. Hence, Arihant Enterprises has launched Adschela.com to automate the whole process of ad booking and to increase its reach countrywide.

Adschela.com is a completely automated online platform where one can book classified ads in all local/national newspapers of India. The portal has been developed as such to make ad booking as smooth as it can get .

No extra cost has been charged to the clients for this online service.
